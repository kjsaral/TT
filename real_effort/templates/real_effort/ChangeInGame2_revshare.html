The Rules of the Group Account:

You and the other two members are working under the same framework with the choice to submit tasks to the individual account or group account.

Every correct task submitted by a group member in the group account generates $0.18 to the group and all group earnings are split equally. This means that you and the other group members each earn $0.06 for every correct task submitted into the group account. It does not matter who submits the correct task in the group account. All earnings are divided equally between all members.

The more the group submits to the group account, the more all group members earn from the group account.

Examples:

If you submit no tasks in the group account, but the other members submit enough to earn $3.60, you would earn $1.20 from the group account.

If the others submit no tasks in the group account, but you put in enough for the group to earn $3.60, you still earn $1.20 from the group account.

Suppose instead that you and the members of your group together submit a total of 50 correct tasks in the group account. The group earns 50*$0.18 = $9.00, and each group member earns their equal share of this amount, $3.00. Notice that you would receive $3.00 from the group account whether or not you submitted any of your correct tasks into the group account.
