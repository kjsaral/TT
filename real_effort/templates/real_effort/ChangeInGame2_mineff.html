The Rules of the Group Account:

You and the other two members are working under the same framework with the choice to submit tasks to the individual account or group account.

The minimum number of correct tasks submitted by a group member in the group account generates $0.18 to all group members. This means that if you submit the lowest number of correct tasks, you and the other group members earn $0.18 for every correct task you submitted into the group account. It does not matter if the group members submitted more than this minimum number of correct answers. Only the minimum number will earn the full payment of $0.18 per task. All tasks submitted above the minimum amount earn 0.

The higher the minimum number of tasks submitted in the group account, the more all group members earn from the group account.

Examples:

If you submit no tasks in the group account, the minimum number submitted is 0 (set by you). All group members would earn 0.

If the at least one of the other members submits no tasks in the group account, but you submit 20 tasks, the minimum number submitted is 0 (set by others). All group members would earn 0.
